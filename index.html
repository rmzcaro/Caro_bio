About Carolina "Caro" Ramirez

My name is Carolina Ramirez, I am originally from Coahuila, Mexico. I enjoy dancing, writing, being outside and eating. I have tried to all of these at the same time, without luck, so far. I've lived in Houston for four years and don't mind the heat. I try to scout out the best places to eat everywhere I go - yelp and a growing network of foodies have been my loves.
